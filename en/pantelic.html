ART DIRECTOR OF 46. FEST

JUGOSLAV PANTELIĆ

46. FEST is Juglosav Panetlić's third as an art director. Jugoslav Pantelić is the director of Jugoslovenska kinoteka, and he was the executive director of television, a long time editor of movie programme and a movie critic on TV Studio B.

Jugoslav Pantelić is the director of Jugoslovenska kinoteka, and he was the executive director of television, a long time editor of movie programe and a movie critic on TV Studio B. He is a member of the European movie academy (since 2012. ) and the current art director of Fest. His portfolio includes a large number of TV and radio shows (Cinema Club,Tačno u podne, Pop Corn, Hronika Festa, Hronika Festivala autorskog filma...), specials and interviews from the world of seventh art, as well as two decades of reporting from the most important world film events.

Pantelić hold a bachelor's degree in communicologist and the author of hundreds of exclusive TV interviews with the most important domestic and international movie authors. He was a board member of Fest on two occasions and presided the Council of the Belgrade international movie festival in 2005. He was the selector of movie festivals in Paris, Novi Sad and on Palić, as well as the jury member of domestic movie festivals.

He is the winner of city of Belgrade prize in the field of journalism for 2012. as well as the silver plaque of the Fest Directorate, and a large number of letters of thanks from domestic movie festivals. Jugoslav Pantelić is the initiator of the "Nebojša Đukelić" prize, which is awarded by the movie critics to the best movie of the Fest.

He was born and lives in Belgrade.
