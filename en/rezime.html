46. FEST and ten exciting festival days of the largest movie festival in Serbia are behind us. Festival was opened on 23. february with the screening of the award winning film "Three bilboards outside Ebbing Missouri" by Martin McDonagh, and closed on 4. march with the screening of the film "Face" by Malgorzata Szumowska, which won the Silver bear jury award.

During the ten days in 12 halls all across Belgrade the audience had the chance to see 116 premiere screenings of world cinematography films during the 418 screenings. Among them were the winners of prestigious awards such as "Square" by Ruben Ostlund, winner of Golden palm, or "Shape of the water", the winner of Golden lion in Venice, Holywood hits such as "I, Tonya", "Roman J. Israel, ESQ" and "Lady bird", films by well known authors like Fatih Akin, Roman Polanski, Paul Thomas Anderson and Chen Kaige, as well as the undiscovered pearls of world and European cinematography. FEST audience had the change to see 10 Oscar nominated films, out of which six winers - "Shape of the water" won the best picture Oscar, best director, best score, best set design. "Three bilboards outside Ebbing Missouri" won best actress (Frances McDormand) and best supporting actor (Sam Rockwell). "I, Tonya" won the best supporting actress ("Allison Janney"), "Secret love" won the best adaptation, "Phantom thread" won the best costume and "A fantastic woman" won the best foreign movie Oscar.

Festival hosted over 117 guests, among them the Oscar winning Stefan Ruzowitzky, italian director Paolo Genovese, Hungarian director Kornel Mundruco, Macedonian director Milić Mančevski, actress Stoya, as well as regional stras - Sebastian Kavaca, Goran Bogdan, Janko Popovi&ccacute; Volarić, Nevi Marasović... Legendary Hungarian director Ištvarn Sabo recieved the award Beogradski pobednik for extraordinary contribution to the film art, and the Neda Arnerić won the same award. Russian director Karen Shakhnazarov won the Beogradski pobednik for his work. During the festival, nine press conferences were held with our guests, which gave 72 interviews.

In the three competition selections 19 movies were screened and on two festival nights the best recieved awards. Five premiere films were screened in the Serbian film selection - "Izgrednici", "Ederlezi rising", "Banditi u potrazi za mamom", "O bubicama i herojima" and "Horizonti". The absolute victory in this category went to the movie "Ederlezi rising", which won the best movie award, best screenplay award (Lazar Bodro&zzaron;a), best male lead (Sebastijan Kavaca), best female lead (Stoya). Kosta Ristić won the jury award for the film "Banditi u potrazi za mamom", and Kristijan Garip was pronounced best debutant in the same film. Film "Izgrednici" won the screenplay award for Đorđe Milosavljević, as well as the special photography award for Miladin Čolaković

In the Granice selection, Rudiger Zuhsland triumphed with the movie "Hitler's Holywood", while Milad Alami with the film "Charmer" won the main competition programme, winning the best movie and best screenplay award. The best director award went to Paul Schrader for the film "First reformed", the best debutant was Natalia Gargoila, film "Hunting season", best male actor award went to Adel Karam in the film "The insult", and the whole "Angels wear white" ensembe won the best female actress award.

The most wanted film by the audience was the "Phantom thread" by Paul Thomas Anderson, which won the Gorki list most wanted movie award.

For all those that didn't see their favourites, an extra festival day was organised, FEST encore or postFESTum, which was held in six halls all across city. The next, 47. FEST will be held from 22. february to 3. march 2019.
