FFEST audience had the chance to see six Oscar winning films

The film with the most nominations was "Shape of the water" by Guilermo del Toro, thirteen, and won the prize in four categories, including the most prestigious one - for the best movie.

46. FEST was officially closed last night, and at the same time, in Los Angeles, the biggest movie event of the year was held - Oscar 2018. FEST audience had the chance to see 10 films nominated for the prestigious Academy award, out of which six films won in ten categories.

Guilermo del Toro's "Shape of the water" had the most nominations, thirteen, and it won in four categories, including the most prestigious one - for the best movie. Del Toro won the best director Oscar, and made history as the third Mexican director to triumph in this category, after Alfonso Cuar&ocaute;n and Alejandro G. Iñ´rritu. Alexandar Desplat won the best score award and the film also won the best set design award.

Frances McDormand won the best female lead award for the film "Three bilboards outside Ebbing Missouri", and her costar Sam Rockwell won the best supporting actor award. Allison Janney won the best supporting actress for the film "I, Tonya".

Chilean film "Fantastic woman" won the best foreign movie award, Mark Bridges won the best costume award for the "Phantom thread" and James Ivory became the eldest Oscar winner in history, winning the best adapted screenplay for the "Secret love", at the age of 89.
