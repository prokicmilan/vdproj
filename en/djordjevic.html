SELECTOR OF PROGRAMME THRILLS & KILLS, MICROWAVE AND GRANICE MLADEN ĐORĐEVIĆ

Born in 1978. in Belgrade, graduated from Tenth Belgrade grammar school, and got his bachelors degree in movie and tv direction on Faculty of Dramatic arts in Belgrade with a feature film MADE IN SERBIA which had its premiere on FEST in 2005. and then went into cinema and DVD distribution. He is the author of multiple short feature films (ŽIVI MRTVACI, GLAD, PENA ZA BRIJANJE) as well as mid and full length documentaries (ALI HAMADOVA PRIČA, PRAVO KROZ VETAR, RATNICI u produkciji ARHITEL), which have been shown successfully both domestically and internationally.

He is the director of RTS show "Desnica u Srbiji između dva rata", which had its premiere in 2003. Over the period of a couple of years he worked on many RTS projects as an external associate, in the role of screenwriter and director.

His latest feature film is ŽIVOT I SMRT PORNO BANDE (2009. FEST premiere, production Baš čelik, with support of Secretariate of Culture of city of Belgrade and Ministry of Culture). Controversial PORNO BANDA was shown on domestic festivals of feature film and on forty foreign movie festivals. The movie won a few domestic and fourteen foreign awards.

Regarding the production of Movie news, he worked as the director and screenwriter of the Lepenski vir movie, as well as the screenwriter of two episodes in the series about Josip Broz Tito.

The phase of postproduction of omnibus Equals, composed of six stories is in progress (directors: Dejan Karaklajić, Darko Longulov, Igor Stoimenov, Miloš Petričić, Ivica Vidanović, Mladen Djordjević), in which Mladen Đorđević is signed as the director and coscreenwriter of the story "Sa&sscaron;a". Festival and cinema premiere of the movie is expected by the end of the year.

Đorđević is the owner and director of production company Corona Film, which has multiple short feature films in its portfolio. In production of this house, author is preparing two new movie projects, which were given support at the Movie center of Serbia contest. One is a documentary movie and series "GASTARBAJTERSKE PRIČE", and the other one is a short feature movie "NAZIM I MILUTIN".
