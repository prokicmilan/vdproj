Skarlet Johanson

Johansson began acting during childhood, after her mother started taking her to auditions. She made her professional acting debut at the age of eight in the off-Broadway production of "Sophistry" with Ethan Hawke, at New York's Playwrights Horizons. She would audition for commercials but took rejection so hard her mother began limiting her to film tryouts. She made her film debut at the age of nine, as John Ritter's character's daughter in the fantasy comedy North (1994). Following minor roles in Just Cause (1995), as the daughter of Sean Connery and Kate Capshaw's character, and If Lucy Fell (1996), she played the role of Amanda in Manny and Lo (1996). Her performance in Manny and Lo garnered a nomination for the Independent Spirit Award for Best Lead Female, and positive reviews. After that came hits like Lost in translation, Her, The Nanny Diaries, The Prestige, Love in Barcelona and couple of movies from the Marvel series.

Born: 22.11.1984.
Location: New York, America
Active period: 1994-
Height: 1,6 m
Husband: Romen Dorijak
Most famous for: Avengers, Lost in translation, Her, Lusy
